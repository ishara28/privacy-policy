Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 13, 2026

Meal Diary respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the Meal Diary mobile application.

Information Collection and Use

Meal Diary is designed as a simple meal tracking application. Meal entries and related information are stored locally on your device using local database technologies.

At this time, Meal Diary does not collect, transmit, or store your personal information on external servers.

Data Storage

All meal data entered into the application is stored locally on your device. If the application is uninstalled, locally stored data may be removed from your device.

Data Sharing

Meal Diary does not sell, rent, share, or disclose your data to third parties.

Children’s Privacy

Meal Diary is not directed toward children under the age of 13. The application does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Third-Party Services

The application may use platform services provided by Google Play or Expo for app distribution and functionality. These services may collect technical information according to their own privacy policies.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Any future changes will be reflected on this page.
